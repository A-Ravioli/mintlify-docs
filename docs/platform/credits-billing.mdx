# Credits & Billing

Frontier uses a credit-based billing system. Purchase credits to pay for training runs and other platform usage.

## Purchasing Credits

1. Navigate to `/signal/billing`
2. Select a credit package:
   - **$50** - 50 credits + 5 bonus credits
   - **$100** - 100 credits + 15 bonus credits
   - **$250** - 250 credits + 50 bonus credits
   - **$500** - 500 credits + 150 bonus credits
3. Click "Purchase"
4. Complete Stripe Checkout
5. Credits are added automatically after payment

<Note>
  Bonus credits are included with each purchase. The more you buy, the more bonus credits you get!
</Note>

## Viewing Your Balance

Check your credit balance at:
- Dashboard (`/signal`) - Shows current balance
- Billing page (`/signal/billing`) - Detailed balance and transaction history

## Transaction History

View all your credit purchases and spending:
- Transaction date
- Amount purchased
- Payment status
- Credit balance after transaction

## Auto-Recharge

Set up automatic credit purchases when your balance gets low:

1. Navigate to `/signal/billing`
2. Click "Set up Auto-Recharge"
3. Choose a threshold (e.g., when balance < $10)
4. Select an amount to purchase automatically
5. Enable auto-recharge

<Info>
  Auto-recharge ensures you never run out of credits. You can disable it at any time.
</Info>

## Credit Usage

Credits are automatically deducted when you:
- Start a training run
- Use compute resources
- Access premium features

## Pricing

Training costs vary based on:
- Model size
- Training duration
- GPU type used
- Dataset size

See your run details for exact costs per training job.

## Payment Methods

Frontier accepts:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- All payments processed securely through Stripe

<Warning>
  All charges are in USD. International cards are accepted.
</Warning>

## Refunds

- Credits are non-refundable once purchased
- Unused credits remain in your account indefinitely
- Contact support for billing issues

## Troubleshooting

### Credits not updating after payment
- Check Stripe webhook configuration
- Verify payment was successful in Stripe dashboard
- Refresh your browser and check balance again

### Payment failed
- Verify your card details are correct
- Check your card has sufficient funds
- Try a different payment method

### Missing transaction
- Check transaction history
- Verify webhook is configured correctly
- Contact support with transaction ID
